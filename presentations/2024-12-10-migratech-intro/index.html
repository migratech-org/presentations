Why start MigraTech?

What is it about?

First of all Community.

We want migrants who live here to:

  • Connect with each other
  • Share experience
  • Build technology
  • and promote their work

Examples of similar communities:

  • Black in Tech: Focus on African descent
  • Girls who Code: Girls, Women and Non-Binary
  • Out in Tech: LGBTQ+ Community
  • Indigitize: Indigenous community in the US
  • Hack the Hood: Black, Latinx, Indigenous, AAPI
  • Techqueria: Serving Latin√© community
  • ...and there are many more!

But then Why MigraTech?

Migrants have different challenges

  • Not knowing where things are, and what exists
  • Migrant groups are divided
  • Loneliness and Network
  • Formalities: Language, Visa, Taxes, etc
  • Labour laws & Politics

This is what MigraTech is planning to build

  • Regular meetups and workshops
  • Network of migrant professionals
  • Giving back to the community
  • Knowledge sharing
  • Exposure and work promotion
