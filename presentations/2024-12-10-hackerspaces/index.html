What is a hackerspace?

Hackerspaces are community-operated physical places, where people can meet and work on their projects.

Source: https://wiki.hackerspaces.org/Berlin

It is about people.

c-base - our host

  • This is one of the oldest Hackerspaces in Berlin, founded in 1995.
  • People here work on different project, from software to electronics to woodworking.
  • People here sometimes just do a music jam.

CCC - Chaos Computer Club

  • By far the biggest organisaiton in Germany.
  • Has branchers (called ERFA-Kreise) in many cities around Germany.
  • Organise the biggest nerd-event in Germany - The Congress: https://events.ccc.de/congress/
  • They have a huge library of talks - https://media.ccc.de/

Benefits of joining a Hackerspace?

  • You get access to a wide network of people
  • People are knowledgable
  • Opportunities to join projects
  • You know about things like the prototypefund
  • People can relate to your nerdy thoughts
  • Political initiatives
  • Access to resources (hardware and knowledge-bases)

What is the Hacker Community like?

Hard to answer. People are very different and diverse. One could say:

  • Friendly
  • Inclusive for disabilities, LGBTQ, foreigners etc.
    • All Creatures welcome
  • Some people are quite introverted
  • Some are politically active people
  • Love for open-source
